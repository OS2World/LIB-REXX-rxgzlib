        Archive Filename: rxGZlib
       Short Description: OS/2 REXX callable procedures to GZIP strings and files
        Long Description: rxGZlib.dll is an OS/2 REXX callable dynamic link library
that contains GZIP procedures. These procedures will deflate and inflate files; and
store results using a GZIP.EXE compatible format. In addition, ZLIB formatted
compression, and crc-32 checksums, are supported.
